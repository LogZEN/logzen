About

pyLogView

pyLogView is a next-generation web-based syslog viewer.

It uses an elasticsearch backend to store syslog messages allowing you to search very efficienty through millions of events. A customizable dashboard allows you to display the most important information at a glance. By evaluating similar events we allow you to recognize the most important log messages, detect root cases and get a new view of the events in your network.
